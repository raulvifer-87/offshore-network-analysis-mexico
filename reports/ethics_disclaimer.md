# Ethics Disclaimer

The inclusion of a person, company or entity in offshore leaks databases does not imply illegality, criminal responsibility, administrative responsibility or wrongdoing.

This project is intended for academic, methodological and data analysis purposes. Its goal is to build a reproducible workflow to identify potential matches and relational patterns in public data.

All matches must be treated as preliminary until manually validated with additional sources, such as public registries, corporate documents, official resolutions, verified journalistic sources or institutional records.

This project avoids publishing sensitive personal data, private addresses or unauthorized private datasets.

When controlled lists of agents, companies or institutions are used, those datasets must remain outside the public repository.
